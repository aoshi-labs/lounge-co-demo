The Intelligent Journal & Tracker

The journal writes itself.

Band photos, voice, and room context become structured tasting history—without a keyboard.

Label-to-Data

Photograph the band. Photograph the glass. Sterlon reads the wrapper, identifies the batch, parses the label, and auto-populates the flavor wheel — leather, peat, vanilla, spice — without you typing a word.

Voice-Activated Tasting

Tap the mic and talk. “I’m smoking an Oliva Serie V with some Eagle Rare. The draw’s a bit tight, but the cherry notes are hitting perfectly.” Sterlon transcribes, structures it into fields, logs the environment, and updates your palate profile.
