Owner Bio

Gerald opened Lounge & Co. in June 2019 after twenty years in hospitality and a lifelong love of fine tobacco. He believes a lounge is a refuge — a quiet hour for serious conversation and an honest pour. He personally curates the cigar program and hosts every Saturday's pairing flight.

Lounge Philosophy

"Members first. Craft over hype. Quiet over loud. Lounge & Co. is a place where people who care about the details come together, leave their phones in the drawer, and talk to each other again."
